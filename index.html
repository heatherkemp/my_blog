Niagara Falls, Ontario

We recently visited the magnificent Niagara Falls and stayed on the Canadian side, which was a lot of fun. There's a boat ride that will take you down close to both the American Falls and the Horseshoe Falls and you get soaked by the mist from the force of the water gushing over the cliffs. It's just an amazing site to see.

Dunn's River Falls, Jamaica

We visited Jamaica on our honeymoon last September and took a day trip to Ocho Rios to see the famed Dunn's River Falls. It is an absolutely spectacular site! The big thing you do there is start from the bottom of the falls, which go all the way to the beach, and climb all the way to the top. It's roughly 600 feet to the top. Along the way we got to slide into pools of water and play in mini-waterfalls.
